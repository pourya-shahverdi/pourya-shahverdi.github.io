## IRB# FY2026-297 — Interaction with Social Robots

Researchers at Oakland University are inviting adults to participate in a research study about how people respond emotionally during social interactions with social robots.

### What will you do?
- Interact with a social robot in short role-playing scenarios
- Complete questionnaires about your character and experience
- Be audio and video recorded during the session

### Study requirements
- 18 years of age or older
- Able to read and speak English
- Willing to be audio and video recorded
- Able to attend an in-person session at Oakland University
- This study takes about 45–60 minutes

### Participant benefits
- Contribute to research on social robots and human emotions
- Help improve future AI technologies
- Receive $15 for participation

### Contact and registration
Pourya Shahverdi | shahverdi@oakland.edu  
Dr. Geoffrey Louie | louie@oakland.edu

To register, please use the calendar below or contact the researchers directly. In-person sessions take place at Oakland University — Pawley Hall #228.
