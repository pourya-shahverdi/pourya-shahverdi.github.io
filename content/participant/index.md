## Schedule an appointment

To publish your Google Calendar on this page:

1. Open your Google Calendar.
2. Click the three dots next to the calendar you want to share and choose Settings and sharing.
3. Make the calendar public.
4. In the Integrate calendar section, copy the embed code.
5. Replace the placeholder below with your calendar embed code.
